############################
This is an attempt to make an easy to use Day Tracker for the world of Greyhawk in the Roll20 API. 
It consolidates the day of the week month and the year and adds info about what moon phase each of 
the 2 moons are in, the tide and its effects, a link to a handout with a downloaded Oerth Calendar 
and a way for the GM to discreetly increase the day. My campaign started on the first of Goodmonth 
and I was on the in game when this was written, thus the start date.
